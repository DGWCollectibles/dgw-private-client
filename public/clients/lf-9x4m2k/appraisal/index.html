Scope of Appraisal

This appraisal covers 25 works of art purchased from Wentworth Gallery, comprising limited edition prints, bronze sculptures, and original paintings. Values provided represent suggested auction estimates based on current secondary market conditions, comparable sales data, and the appraiser's professional judgment.

Important Market Context: Wentworth Gallery operates in cruise ship and mall retail settings where primary market prices significantly exceed secondary market values. This is typical of the decorative art market. The estimates herein reflect achievable auction values, not original retail or replacement values. Secondary market values for gallery-sold decorative art typically realize 10–35% of original retail pricing.

Methodology & Limiting Conditions

Valuation Methodology

Values were determined using the market data approach, considering: (1) recent auction results for comparable works by the same artists; (2) edition size and position within the edition; (3) subject matter desirability and collector demand; (4) medium (original works, bronzes, and limited editions valued accordingly); (5) condition assumptions based on provenance from a reputable gallery; and (6) current market conditions for secondary decorative art.

Limiting Conditions

This appraisal is provided for estate planning and auction consignment purposes only. Values assume works are in good condition consistent with their age and medium. Physical inspection was not conducted; values may be subject to revision upon examination. Auction results are not guaranteed and depend on market conditions, buyer interest, and presentation at time of sale. This appraisal does not constitute an offer to purchase.
